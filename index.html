Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

Sampleapp ("the App") is built with a "Privacy by Design" philosophy. We believe your security analysis should remain private, which is why we utilize on-device processing and minimize data collection.

1. High-Risk Permissions

To provide comprehensive protection, Sampleapp requires the QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission.

Purpose:

This allows the App to inventory all applications installed on your device.

Necessity:

This access is essential for the App’s core functionality—identifying malware, detecting spyware, and analyzing the risk level of permissions requested by other apps on your device.

2. On-Device Processing

Local Security Analysis

All scanning of your installed apps and their associated permissions is performed entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your app list or security analysis results to any external servers.

Zero Network Exposure

Your device's security profile remains your own. No scanning data is ever uploaded to a cloud or shared with third parties.

3. Data Collection & Storage

  • User Consent: We store a record of your digital acknowledgement of this policy. This record is stored securely via Zoho WorkDrive.
  • Crash Reporting: Anonymous technical reports (stack traces) are sent to Google Play Android Vitals to help us fix bugs and ensure stability.

4. Transparency

You can manage or disable system-level diagnostic sharing at any time through your device settings under Settings > Google > Usage & diagnostics.
